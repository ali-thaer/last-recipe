Why This Matters:

“To me, it’s sort of funny that wasting food is not taboo. It’s one of the last environmental ills that you can just get away with.”

- Jonathan Bloom

Throughout the world, food waste is on the rise. When food is thrown away and rots, the greenhouse gas, methane is produced, contributing to our current climate crisis. Furthermore, food waste results in the excessive production of food, which utilizes water, fuel, and energy.

How Does Last Recipe Solve This Issue?

Last Recipe aims to reduce the impact of food waste by providing users with an alternative option, rather than throwing away food: recipes that feature the main ingredient as the food they need to discard.

We hope that through this reduction in food waste, the environmental damage from the activity can be minimized.
